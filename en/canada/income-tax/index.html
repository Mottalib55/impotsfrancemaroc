Complete Guide to Canadian Taxation

Canada has a two-tier tax system with both federal and provincial taxes. Social contributions are relatively low compared to other developed countries, making Canada an attractive destination for workers.

Key Tax Credits and Deductions

  • Basic Personal Amount: CAD 15,705 (federal), CAD 11,865 (Ontario)
  • RRSP: Contributions deductible up to 18% of income (max ~CAD 31,560)
  • TFSA: Tax-free savings account (contribution limit ~CAD 7,000/year)
  • GST/HST Credit: Refundable credit for low-to-moderate income
  • Canada Child Benefit: Up to CAD 7,437 per child annually
