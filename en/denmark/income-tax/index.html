Personal Allowance (Personfradrag)

2026 Allowance: DKK 49,700 per year

This allowance reduces the tax base for bundskat and kommuneskat.

Key Tax Deductions

  • Beskaftigelsesfradrag: Employment deduction (max ~DKK 45,100)
  • Pendlerfradrag: Commuting costs (beyond 24 km)
  • Rentefradrag: Interest on loans is deductible
  • Fagforeningskontingent: Union membership fees
  • Ratepension: Pension savings (up to DKK 63,100)
