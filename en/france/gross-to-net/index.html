Social Contributions in France

Non-Executive: approximately 22% of gross salary is deducted for social contributions.

Executive: approximately 25% of gross salary due to higher supplementary retirement contributions.

Income Tax: calculated with the family quotient system and progressive brackets (0% to 45%).

10% Deduction: automatically applied to taxable income for professional expenses.
