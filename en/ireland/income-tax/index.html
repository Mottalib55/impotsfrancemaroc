Complete Guide to Irish Taxation

Ireland has an attractive tax system with one of the lowest corporate tax rates in Europe (12.5%), which attracts many multinationals. For individuals, the system combines Income Tax, USC, and PRSI.

Key Tax Deductions

  • Pension contributions: Up to 40% of income depending on age (max EUR 115,000)
  • Medical expenses: Non-reimbursed medical costs (20% credit)
  • Home Carer Credit: EUR 1,800 if spouse is homemaker
  • Rent Tax Credit: Up to EUR 750 for renters
  • Flat Rate Expenses: Fixed deductions by profession

Special Tax Reliefs

  • SARP (Special Assignee Relief Programme): 30% income exemption for qualifying assignees
  • R&D Tax Credit: 25% credit for research and development activities
  • Start-up Relief: 3-year corporation tax exemption for new companies
