Key Tax Deductions & Credits

  • Algemene heffingskorting: General tax credit (~EUR 3,362 max, decreases with income)
  • Arbeidskorting: Employment tax credit (~EUR 5,532 max)
  • Hypotheekrenteaftrek: Mortgage interest deduction
  • 30% ruling: 30% tax exemption for qualified expats (5 years)
  • Reisaftrek: Commuting expense deduction
